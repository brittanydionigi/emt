Resucitation

Equipment:

  • oxygen tank
  • BVM
  • tubing to connect oxygen & BVM
  • OPA
  • AED with electrodes & pads
  • Practice giving a breath to the dummy

Fab Five

  • BSI
  • scene safety
  • nature of illness
  • # of patients
  • additional resources

AVPU

  • Getting a general impression
  • Sir are you ok, are you ok?
  • Pinching the ear and trap
  • Patient is unresponsive, starting ALS
  • Palpating the carotid pulse for 5-10 seconds while I look, listen and feel for breathing
  • Partner start compressions

Cycle 1

  • Sizing an OPA, measuring from the corner of the mouth to the bottom of the ear
  • and inserting upside-down, rotating 180 degrees until the OPA is resting on the lips
  • Give two breaths - if no chest rise, readjust: Readjusting to ensure adequate chest rise

Cycle 2

  • Open the AED & connect electrodes
  • Give two breaths

Cycle 3

  • Place right pad - make sure wires aren't in anyone's way
  • Give two breaths

Cycle 4

  • Place left pad - make sure it's placed at the BOTTOM of the dummy
  • Give two breaths

Cycle 5

  • Turn the AED on
  • Clear the patient to analyze
  • Press analyze
  • Rotate positions while AED is analyzing
  • ALL CLEAR OF THE PATIENT
  • Press shock
  • Restart compressions
