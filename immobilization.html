CSM

  • Partner please take c-spine
  • Can you wiggle your fingers and toes for me?
  • Checking radial pulse - which hand am I touching?
  • Checking pedal pulse - which foot am I touching?

Applying C-Collar

  • Sizing and applying a c-collar, measuring fingers from the nape of the neck to the jaw line Apply the collar

Positioning KED

  • On my partner's count we're going to lean you forward slightly Hold chest and back to lean forward
  • Place KED behind patient, set leg straps free, pull ked up into armpits, assess the head for voids
  • On my partner's count we'll lean you back again Stabilize neck while leaning patient back

Affixing straps

  • Middle & bottom straps *tight*
  • Leg straps, pad the groin, *tight*
  • Top strap, loose
  • Assess that all straps are tight, if not, redo them in the order they were applied

Stabilizing the head

  • Apply forehead strap
  • Apply chin strap

CSM

  • Can you wiggle your fingers and toes for me?
  • Checking radial pulse - which hand am I touching?
  • Checking pedal pulse - which foot am I touching?
  • Partner can release c-spine
